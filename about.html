About us

We build the whole product.

We're a small team of engineers who got tired of watching good product ideas fall apart because of handoffs — between hardware and software teams, between designers and manufacturers, between whoever thought of the idea and whoever was supposed to build it.

So we built a practice that handles the whole thing. You bring the problem. We handle the architecture, the hardware, the firmware, the mechanics — and we deliver something that actually works in the real world.

What we do

⚡ Hardware & PCBA

System architecture, schematic capture, PCB layout, component sourcing, prototype assembly, and volume production. We own the full hardware stack.

💾 Firmware & Software

Embedded firmware, BSP, RTOS, IoT connectivity, mobile apps, cloud backends. We write the code that makes the hardware useful.

🔩 Mechanical Design

Enclosures, structural design, thermal management, DFM review, tooling coordination. Products need to survive the real world.

🎨 ID Design (via partner)

Industrial design, CMF, rendering. We coordinate with trusted ID partners when you need it.

How we work

No fixed pricing

Every project is different. You describe what you need, we scope it properly and send a real quote. No price lists, no surprises.

Architecture first

We think about the whole system before touching a schematic. Most expensive mistakes happen in architecture, not manufacturing.

Direct communication

You deal with the engineers, not account managers. Questions get real answers, not status updates.

Delivered, not handed off

We stay involved through production. A working prototype is not the finish line — a reliable, manufacturable product is.
